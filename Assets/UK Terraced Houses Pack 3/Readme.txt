Introduction:
A collection of 6 low poly terraced style houses, absolutely ideal for any background town / city scene. Each house will come with 3 different texture maps along with a mirrored version making a total of 36 different house variations. 

Just drag any of the house prefabs into your project hierarchy to add a house to your scene.

Package Contents:
- 6 unique house models
- 3 unique texture maps for each house (1024x1024 res, Diffuse & Normal maps)
- Mirrored version for each house
- 36 different house variations overall

Triangle Counts:
Between 500 & 1400 Triangles per house
